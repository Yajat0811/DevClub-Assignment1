FAQS

  • What is Covid-19
    Covid 19 is a disease caused by a virus called SARS-CoV-2.Most people with Covid-19 have mild symptoms,but some become
    severly ill.However most people with Covid-19 gets better within weeks of illness,some people experience post covid complications.

  • What are Symptoms and Complications that Covid-19 can cause?
    People with Covid-19 have reported a wide range of symptoms- from mild symptoms to severe illness.Symptoms may appear 2-14 days after exposure to the virus.If you have fever,cough or other symptoms,you might have Covid-19.
